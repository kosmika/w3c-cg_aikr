WORKING DRAFT — sketch   This note is a first, deliberately wide inventory of standardisation activity touching agentic AI on the Web, across W3C, the IETF, ISO/IEC and industry / Linux Foundation efforts. For each effort it records the standard being worked on, its core elements, and a sketch of how it aligns to the AI KR CG's AIAO vocabulary and knowledge-representation layers. It is a scaffold for a fuller, verified map to be developed and published separately.

This is a working draft and a non-normative sketch. Entries reflect the landscape as understood in early 2026 and require verification; several efforts are actively converging or being re-homed between bodies. The alignment column is indicative, not settled.
